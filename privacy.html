Privacy Policy for TaskDHD

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

1. Introduction

TaskDHD ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile and desktop applications.

2.No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or IP address.
  • We do not use third-party tracking or analytics (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel).

3. Local Storage Only

Any data you create within the app (such as your tasks and settings) is stored locally on your own device.

  • This data is stored using browser-based technologies (LocalStorage/IndexedDB) or native mobile storage.
  • This data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • If you delete the app or clear your app data, your tasks will be permanently deleted.

4. Permissions

The app may request standard permissions required for its functionality (such as notifications). These permissions are used solely to provide features on your device and are not used to collect data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app website is hosted using Cloudflare Pages. While we do not collect data, Cloudflare may collect standard web logs (like your IP address) for security and performance purposes. Please refer to Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy for more details.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your contact information, we encourage you to check this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: developer@taskdhd.net
