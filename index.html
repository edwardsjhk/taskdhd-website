TaskDHD

A list & task manager app for people with ADHD

Lots of tasks can be overwhelming. Lots of lists can be overwhelming. Visualising tasks to bring your attention just to what your doing here and now - that can really help. It's helping me with my ADHD everyday, so I wanted to share. Web version here.

See TaskDHD in Action

Priority means BIG

The highest priority item is the biggest! This helps draw your attention to what you need to do without being overwhelmed.

Change Colours!

Tap the colour changer button and easily change between colour presets to hack your brain.

Reusable Task Templates

Save mental energy by creating a colorful repository of your daily routines to instantly throw onto your main list.

Set-and-Forget Automation

Program recurring chores (like weekly laundry) to automatically appear on your list right when you need them.

Visual Time-Blocking

Drag unscheduled tasks into an easy-to-read daily timeline to structure your hours realistically.

Frictionless Rescheduling

Plans change. Use intuitive quick-delay menus to bump a task to tomorrow or next week without feeling guilty.

Out of Sight, Out of Mind

Keep your main list entirely clutter-free by shelving delayed tasks until their designated return date.
